Frequent asked questions (FAQ)

Construindo o Futuro (CF), portugues for ‚ÄúBuilding a Future‚Äù, is an organization founded in Brazil which consists of teenagers who develop social-environmental projects to promote the Sustainable Development Goals in their schools and communities. We help teenagers worldwide to create local embassies, groups of students who get together to solve their community problems.

Ambassadors are local leaders who take the CF to their schools and prepare volunteers to implement and create projects.

There are many benefits of being an ambassador, such as:
- Being able to develop projects for social and environmental causes, transforming your community into a more inclusive and sustainable place
- Exercising your leadership and group work skills
- Being part of a large support network, meeting several teenagers (other volunteers and ambassadors) motivated to build a better world
- Having experiences of volunteer work in the curriculum (although the ambassadors' motivation must be the genuine desire to do good!)

To be an ambassador, one must be, in the year 2021, in High School, 9th or 8th year of Elementary School; be truly willing to build a better world; having availability of at least 4 hours a week for CF activities and, finally, knowing how to work well in groups and respecting different opinions.
